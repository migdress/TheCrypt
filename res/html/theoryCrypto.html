THEORY > Cryptography
In a nutshell, Cryptography is the art of hiding valuable information to unauthorized entities.
check Wikipedia for better understanding.

Terminology

Encoding: It means representing information.

Encrypting: It means protecting information.

Plain-text: The unencrypted information.

Ciphertext: The encrypted information.

Encryption algorithm/Cipher algorithm: A process that executes the necessary instructions for ecrypting and/or decrypting information.

Decryption: It means the recovery of encrypted information with authorized access.

Cryptanalysis: It means the recovery of encrypted information and probably the key(s) without authorized access.
